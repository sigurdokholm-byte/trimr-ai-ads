Know your shape

What makes a diamond face shape?

🔷

Diamond

Wide cheeks · Narrow top & jaw

Forehead

Narrow — narrower than the cheekbones and similar width to the jaw. This creates the "pointed" top of the diamond.

Cheekbones

The widest point of the face by a significant margin. High, prominent, angular cheekbones are the defining feature.

Jaw & Chin

Narrow jaw tapering to a pointed chin — creates the bottom point of the diamond shape.

Goal with Hairstyles

Add width or volume at the forehead, reduce visual width at the cheekbones, and balance the narrow chin.

Top 5 cuts

Best hairstyles for diamond face men

Cuts that add forehead volume and balance the prominent cheekbones. Ranked by AI compatibility score.

Side Part with Volume

93 / 100

A swept side part with volume at the temples and forehead adds width where the diamond face is narrowest. The asymmetry also draws attention away from the cheekbone width.

Medium MaintenanceStraight · WavyVersatile
Tell your barber: "Low-medium fade, hard part on the left, leave 3 inches on top. I'll comb it over to the right with volume at the forehead."

Curtains / Middle Part

91 / 100

Hair parted in the middle and falling to both sides. The width this creates at the temples visually widens the forehead, balancing the wide cheekbones below.

Low MaintenanceStraight · WavyTrendy
Tell your barber: "Curtains cut — keep 3–4 inches on top, light fade on the sides. I'll middle-part it and let it fall naturally."

Textured Fringe

88 / 100

A fringe that falls forward and slightly to the sides adds width at the forehead line — exactly what a diamond face needs. The texture prevents the look from appearing flat.

Low MaintenanceFine · MediumEveryday
Tell your barber: "Textured fringe — fade the sides, leave 2.5 inches on top brushed forward. Scissor texture through the crown."

Quiff

85 / 100

The height and width the quiff adds at the front of the head broadens the narrow forehead visually. Works best when the quiff fans out slightly rather than pointing straight up.

Medium MaintenanceThick · FineSmart
Tell your barber: "Quiff — mid fade, leave 3 inches on top. I'll blow-dry it forward and up with a round brush for width at the front."

Wavy Medium Length

83 / 100

Medium-length hair with natural wave that creates volume at the sides of the head — softening the sharp cheekbones and adding fullness at the temple area near the forehead.

Low Daily StylingWavy · CurlyRelaxed

Diamond face shape questions

Yes — diamond is considered the rarest of the six primary face shapes. It's also often misidentified because the cheekbones need to be significantly wider than both the forehead and jaw to qualify. Many people who think they have a diamond face are actually oval or heart-shaped.
Very short all-over crops can expose the narrow forehead and make it appear even narrower relative to the cheekbones. Crops with a fringe or some texture at the front work much better — they add visual width at the forehead line.
