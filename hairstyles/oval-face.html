Know your shape

What makes an oval face shape?

⬭

Oval

Most common · Most versatile

Forehead

Slightly wider than the jaw — gently curved at the hairline with no harsh corners.

Cheekbones

The widest point of the face, but only marginally — the difference is subtle.

Jaw

Narrower than the forehead with a rounded, tapered shape. No strong angles.

Length vs Width

Face length is about 1.5× the width — longer than it is wide, but balanced.

Top 6 cuts

Best hairstyles for oval face men

Ranked by TRIMR AI compatibility score for the average oval face shape.

Quiff

96 / 100

Voluminous on top, tapered on the sides. The quiff adds height without overwhelming an already-balanced face. Works with thick or fine hair.

Medium Maintenance Thick · Fine Formal · Casual
Tell your barber: "I want a quiff — fade the sides from a #2 up, leave 3-4 inches on top and blow-dry it forward and up with a round brush."

Textured Crop

93 / 100

Short, textured, low maintenance. A fade on the sides with scissor-textured length on top. Effortless and modern — suits almost any lifestyle.

Low Maintenance All Hair Types Everyday
Tell your barber: "Textured crop with a skin fade. Keep 2 inches on top, use scissors to add texture through the crown, no hard part."

Pompadour

91 / 100

The classic pompadour — hair swept back and up at the front. Oval faces carry this with ease. Best with thick hair and a strong-hold pomade.

High Styling Thick Hair Statement
Tell your barber: "Classic pompadour — high fade on the sides, leave 4+ inches on top, I'll style it back and up at home with pomade."

Slick Back

88 / 100

Sleek, professional, and timeless. Push everything backward with a medium-hold product. Oval faces have the balanced proportions to pull this off cleanly.

Low Maintenance Medium–Thick Hair Professional
Tell your barber: "I want a slick-back — taper the sides, leave enough length on top to comb straight back. No fade."

Undercut

87 / 100

Shaved or very short sides with distinctly longer hair on top. Creates strong contrast — works especially well on oval faces because the proportions don't need correcting.

Medium Maintenance Thick Hair Edgy · Modern
Tell your barber: "Disconnected undercut — shave the sides to a #1, leave the top long and square-cut. Hard part optional."

French Crop

84 / 100

Blunt fringe, short-to-medium sides, short top. Clean and effortless. Great for guys who want minimal styling but a proper shape.

Very Low Maintenance Fine · Medium Hair Minimal Styling
Tell your barber: "French crop — fade the sides, leave a short blunt fringe at the front, keep the top around 1.5 inches."
