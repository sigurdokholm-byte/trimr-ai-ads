Know your shape

What makes an oblong face shape?

⬛

Oblong

Long · Narrow · Uniform width

Length vs Width

Face length significantly exceeds width — the face reads as noticeably longer than it is wide. Unlike oval, the length is more extreme.

Width at All Points

Forehead, cheekbones, and jaw are roughly the same width — no one point is dramatically wider than the others. The face is uniformly narrow.

Jaw

Slightly squared or rounded at the chin — less tapered than oval. The jaw length contributes to the elongated appearance.

Goal with Hairstyles

Add visual width, reduce visual length. Avoid height — it elongates further. Favour styles with side volume and a forward-falling fringe.
