Know your shape

What makes a round face shape?

⭕

Round

Soft features · Wide cheeks

Width vs Height

Face width and height are nearly equal — the defining trait of a round face. This is what creates the "soft" appearance.

Cheekbones

Wide and prominent cheekbones — the widest point of the face, with gentle curves on each side.

Jaw

Rounded, soft jawline without strong angles. Chin is curved rather than pointed.

Goal with Hairstyles

Add visual height (makes face appear longer), reduce perceived width (avoid styles that balloon at the sides).

What to avoid

Cuts that don't suit round faces

Round face men should avoid these styles:

✕Bowl cuts — add width across the sides and emphasise the circular shape of the face.
✕Side sweeps with heavy volume — pushing hair to the side without height increases perceived width.
✕Very short all-over cuts (buzz cuts) — removes the ability to add height and leaves the round shape fully exposed.
✕Curly afros without any fade — the sphere shape of a full afro mirrors the face shape, amplifying roundness. A mid or high fade fixes this.
