Know your shape

What makes a heart face shape?

♥

Heart

Wide forehead · Narrow jaw

Forehead

Wide — the widest point of the face. Often has a slightly pointed widow's peak or a rounded hairline that emphasises the width.

Cheekbones

High and relatively prominent, though not as wide as the forehead. They help create the curved "top" of the heart shape.

Jaw & Chin

Narrow and tapers noticeably to a pointed chin — the "bottom" of the heart. Often a delicate, slender jaw.

Goal with Hairstyles

Reduce visual width at the forehead, add width/volume at the jaw level, and balance the pointed chin.

Best hairstyles for heart face men

Side Swept Fringe

94 / 100

A fringe swept to one side breaks up the wide forehead, reducing its visual dominance. The side movement also creates asymmetry which distracts from the straight forehead line.

Low MaintenanceStraight · FineEveryday

Textured Messy Top

91 / 100

Longer, messy, textured hair on top with low-to-mid faded sides. The texture and natural movement of the hair softens the forehead width without the rigidity of a swept style.

Low MaintenanceAll Hair TypesCasual

Longer Styles

Medium to longer hair (3–5 inches) that falls naturally. The length draws the eye downward, balancing the wider top. Works especially well with wavy or curly hair that creates volume at ear level.

Curtains

86 / 100

Middle-parted hair falling to both sides. The curtains style draws width downward and outward from the forehead, and the parted middle visually narrows the forehead width.

Low MaintenanceStraight · WavyTrendy

Low Fade + Beard
