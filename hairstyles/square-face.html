Know your shape

What makes a square face shape?

⬜

Square

Strong angles · Defined jaw

Forehead

Wide and straight across — similar width to the jaw. A flat, defined hairline rather than a curved one.

Jaw

The defining feature — wide, angular, and strong. The jaw width is very close to the forehead width, creating a box-like outline.

Cheekbones

Prominent but not dramatically wider than the jaw or forehead — the face width stays similar from top to bottom.

Goal with Hairstyles

Soften the angular jaw and add length. Avoid styles that emphasise the boxy, flat-top silhouette.

Best hairstyles for square face men

Cuts that soften angles, add length, and work with the natural strength of a square face. Ranked by AI compatibility score.

Textured Fringe

94 / 100

A longer fringe falling forward or slightly to the side breaks up the flat forehead line and draws attention away from the jaw's width. One of the most face-softening cuts for square faces.

Low MaintenanceFine · MediumEveryday
Tell your barber: "Mid fade on the sides, leave the top 2.5 inches — push it forward as a textured fringe. Scissor work on top for movement."

Side Part

91 / 100

A clean side part introduces asymmetry that softens the symmetrical boxiness of a square face. Can go from casual to formal. Works with both straight and slightly wavy hair.

Medium MaintenanceAll Hair TypesProfessional
Tell your barber: "Low fade on the sides with a hard part on the left. Leave 2.5–3 inches on top to comb over to the right."

Quiff

89 / 100

Adds height above the forehead, elongating the face and reducing the emphasis on the jaw's width. A quiff on a square face reads as powerful without looking harsh.

Medium MaintenanceThick · FineVersatile
Tell your barber: "Taper fade on the sides, 3 inches on top. I'll blow-dry it forward and up — no hard part needed."

Ivy League

87 / 100

A preppy, clean-cut style — slightly longer on top than a crew cut, combed to the side. The softness of the style contrasts well with a strong jaw. Classic and low-effort to maintain.

Low MaintenanceStraight · WavySmart Casual
Tell your barber: "Ivy League — clean taper, a bit more length on top than a crew cut. I'll comb it to the side."

Longer Wavy Styles

85 / 100

Caesar Cut

82 / 100
